02 —

Thesis

Deploying agents is a business problem before it's a technical one.

Legacy systems. Fragmented data. Undocumented workflows. The need to keep the business running during the transformation.

Every prior wave of enterprise technology produced its own generation of services firms that learned how to ship it. clus.ai is built for this wave, with a structural difference: a network, not a bench.

We deploy operators who ran the business before they automated it. CFOs who built FP&A agents for their own teams. Heads of revenue who automated their own GTM motion. M&A leads who integrated companies before integrating systems.

That's the difference between a deck and a deployment.

03 —

How we work

Three steps. 10 to 80 days.

i.

Scope

A senior partner runs a tight diagnostic that identifies which workflows are agent-ready today, which need data or process work first, and which should not be touched yet. Fixed fee. Concrete report. Two weeks.

ii.

Match

From an invitation-only network of senior operators, we assemble a small pod matched to your industry, your stack, and your stage. Pods embed for 10 to 80 days. No ramp. No bench tax.

iii.

Ship

The pod works in production from week one. The deliverable is an agent your team owns. The engagement ends when the target metric moves and your team can run it without us.
